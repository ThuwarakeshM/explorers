About Adventure Explorers

Adventure Explorers is an organization that brings all adventure sports together and stands as your partner for any adventure you are looking for in Sri Lanka.

As we based in Kitulgala, we offer a range of water sports, such as whitewater rafting, waterfall abseiling, canyoning. Further, we also serve as guides for Hiking and Camping for many popular campsites and mountain ranges such as Knuckles, Ella Rock.

The organization is founded by field experts and university graduates in collaboration who share the same interest of eco-tourism. Over time, we have gained associations with other organizations.
