Adventure Explorers Policies and Guidelines

Environmental Protection Policies

  • Adventure Explorers team and its clients shall always act in a way to make its environmental effects to an absolute minimum.

  • The killing of wild animals or removal of plants is strictly prohibited under all circumstances.

  • Polyethene and Plastic usage shall be on an absolute minimum level.

  • Leaving Polyethene, Plastic, Can, or any other belongings in the activity premises is strictly prohibited.

  • Possessing any living or non-living things from the activity premises is strictly prohibited.

  • Writing on rocks or creating signs and marks in activity premises is strictly prohibited.

Behavioral and Cultural Policies

  • Participants are expected to behave in a friendly manner towards each other.

  • Participants shall not consume, or not under the control of alcohol or any other abusive drug during the activity.

  • In the event of a conflict, or critical decision-making participants shall always obey the decision made by the guide in charge.

  • Adventure Explorers will not defend you for breaching the governing laws of the country.

  • Participants who aren’t deliberately following the instructions of the instructor or breaching our policy guidelines may subject to termination of the activity contract at any time.

  • Harassment in general, sexual harassment in special towards another participant is strictly prohibited.

Safety Guidelines

  • Adventure explorers will equip everyone with proper safety measures before every activity.

  • Adventure explorers will not allow anyone to participate in its activities, if the participant do not follow the safety instructions of the instructor.

  • Adventure Explorers will provide basic first aid requirements.

  • Adventure Explorers will not provide special medications that might be a unique requirement for specific participants.

  • Participants may be eligible for a discount depending on the number of participants and special offers.

Program Eligibility Guidelines

  • Participants are expected to be in good physical and mental health.

  • Participants should not be under the control of alcohol or any other abusive drug.

  • Participants should satisfy the minimum age requirement, which is 14, if not specified.

  • Pregnant ladies are not allowed to participate in adventures. However, they may be eligible for other events and activities organized by adventure explorers.

General Policies

  • Adventure explorers may use photos and videos during the activity in its publications.

  • Participants may request adventure explorers not to publish their photos and videos.

  • Photos and videos taken by the participants can be published anywhere by the participants without any restriction from adventure explorers.

  • Adventure explorers team requests participants to tag the brand name whenever possible while sharing photos and videos taken during an adventure explorers’ event. However, it is completely optional.
